About Me

Akshith Aithal is a student and emerging author driven by a deep curiosity about the universe, spirituality, and the power of storytelling. Even at a young age, he has developed a strong interest in exploring profound ideas through writing, blending imagination with philosophical and mythological themes. He is the creator of Devi Bhagavatam – The Cosmic Saga, a reimagined narrative inspired by timeless spiritual traditions. Through this series, Akshith seeks to present divine stories in a way that feels vast, immersive, and meaningful to modern readers, while preserving their depth and symbolic richness. Balancing academics with creativity, he continues to develop his voice as a writer, working on ideas that span mythology, philosophy, and original fiction. His goal is not limited to a single work, but to build a body of writing that explores concepts such as creation, cosmic balance, light and darkness, and the deeper nature of existence. Akshith believes that stories are more than entertainment—they are a way to explore the unknown, spark curiosity, and connect with something greater than ourselves.
